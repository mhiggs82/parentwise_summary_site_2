## 1. Site Vision

ParentWise is a sophisticated documentation library providing research-backed parenting book summaries with actionable frameworks. The site serves as a calm, empathetic resource for parents seeking expert guidance during challenging moments.

### Core Principles
- **Empathetic**: Acknowledge parent struggles with warm, non-judgmental design
- **Focused**: Clear visual hierarchy guides attention to actionable content
- **Expert-backed**: Professional polish builds credibility and trust
- **Accessible**: WCAG AAA compliance for inclusive access

### Target Audience
- Parents of children ages 0-12
- Educators and childcare professionals
- Family therapists and counselors
- Anyone seeking evidence-based parenting guidance

---

## 2. Design Identity

### Visual Theme
- **Dark Mode Primary**: Deep navy-black sanctuary (`#0f1116`) with luminous purple accents (`#7c5bff`)
- **Atmosphere**: Sophisticated, calm, and reassuring
- **Typography**: Lexend font stack optimized for long-form reading
- **Glassmorphism**: Subtle blurs and glowing borders for depth
