Demystifying the science of parenting, one term at a time.

C

Co-regulation

The process by which one person's nervous system helps regulate another's, essential for infant development and emotional security.

Cognitive Load

The total amount of mental effort being used in the working memory. In parenting, understanding a child's cognitive load helps in giving appropriate instructions.

N

Neuroplasticity

The brain's ability to reorganize itself by forming new neural connections throughout life, allowing children to learn and adapt from experiences.
